Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the Kraken?

Kraken is an application built to help outbound UAT testing by generating conforming data according to a user-defined schema.

Q: Why I cannot access any resources inside Kraken?

You need log into Kraken with your West Credential.

Q: Why I cannot log into Kraken with West Credential?

Please access West Identity Manger to check your username and password, or contact EIT 800-838-8383 for further support.

Q: What kind of browsers are supported for Kraken?

  • Internet Explorer 11+
  • Google Chrome 29+
  • Mozilla Firefox 24+

Q: What's the Client Navigator?

Client Navigator is the main entrance for all operations supported by Kraken, such as: Add new client, add new schema, add new schema version, edit schema, edit version of schema, save and download generated confirming data.

Q: Why I cannot add new Client, new Schema, new version?

Only user with certain authority has permission to add new client, new schema, new version along with edition ability.

Q: I cannot find any useful information about my question, whom I should contact to?

Please contact to Quality Engineering Innovation team to get more information.
