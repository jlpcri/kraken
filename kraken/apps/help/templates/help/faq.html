Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Kraken?

Kraken is an application built to help outbound UAT testing by generating conforming data according to a user-defined schema.

Q: Why can I not access any resources inside Kraken?

You need log into Kraken with your West Credentials.

Q: Why can I not log into Kraken with West Credentials?

Please access West Identity Manager to check your username and password, or contact EIT 800-838-8383 for further support.

Q: What kind of browsers are supported for Kraken?

  • Internet Explorer 11+
  • Google Chrome 29+
  • Mozilla Firefox 24+

Q: What's the Client Navigator?

The Client Navigator is the main entrance for all operations supported by Kraken, such as: Add new client, add new schema, add new schema version, edit schema, edit version of schema, save and download generated confirming data.

Q: Why cannot I add a new Client, new Schema, or new Version?

Only users with certain authority have permission to add new clients, schemas, and versions, as well as editing ability.

Q: I cannot find any useful information about my question, whom I should contact?

Please contact the Quality Engineering Innovation team for more information.
