Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Kraken?

Kraken is an application built to help outbound UAT testing by generating data that conforms to user-defined schemas.

Q: How do I sign in to Kraken?

You can sign in to Kraken with your West Credentials.

Q: Why aren't my West Credentials working when I try to sign in to Kraken?

Please access West Identity Manager to check your username and password, or contact EIT 800-838-8383 for further support.

Q: What kind of browsers are supported for Kraken?

  • Internet Explorer 11+
  • Google Chrome 29+
  • Mozilla Firefox 24+

Q: What's the Client Navigator?

The Client Navigator is the main entrance for all operations supported by Kraken, such as: Add new client, add new schema, add new schema version, edit schema, edit version of schema, save and download generated confirming data.

Q: How do I create Clients, Schemas, and Versions?

Only certain users have been granted the permission needed to create or edit clients, schemas, and versions.

Q: I have a question not listed here, who can I contact?

Please contact the Quality Engineering Innovation team for more information.
